Albert Einstein

Theoretical physicist

We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them.

  • Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm, Germany
  • Albert Einstein grew up in Munich, where his father founded an electrical engineering company.
  • He was employed at universities in Bern, Zurich, and Prague, and from 1914, in Berlin.
  • In the 1920s, Einstein embarked on the construction of unified field theories, although he continued to work on the probabilistic interpretation of quantum theory, and he persevered with this work in America.
  1. 1920 Barnard Medal for Meritorious Service to Science
  2. 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics
  3. 1921 Matteucci Medal
  4. 1925 Copley Medal
  5. 1926 Gold Medal of the Royal Astronomical Society
  6. 1929 Max Planck Medal
  7. 1935 Franklin Medal

Let us not forget that human knowledge and skills alone cannot lead humanity to a happy and dignified life.

Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile.
